# Designer AI Layer

## Core Objective
Create AI capable of generating professional-grade designs across graphics, video, architecture, and software systems.

## Features
- Graphics and video design: ultra-realistic, adaptive, and automated.
- Architecture and engineering design AI: plans, CAD outputs.
- UX/UI and interactive media design.
- Auto-generation of adaptive templates and assets.
- Integration with Media Engine and Learning AI.

## Input
- Design briefs, templates, references.
- Cultural, historical, and aesthetic inputs.
- AI or user-generated assets for inspiration.

## Output
- Finished design files for graphics, video, architecture.
- Modular templates for future projects.
