# Indigenous / Native American Gothic Movie AI Framework

## Core Objective
Generate Gothic cinematic stories rooted in Native American mythology and ancestral spirits. AI captures cultural tones and emotion depth.

## Features
- Ancestral spirits, land, and blood memory.
- Tension between tribes, spirits, and colonizers.
- Adaptive Gothic tone-catcher.
- AI-generated ambient sounds: forest, wind, fire, ceremonial music.
- Visual AI cinematic engine: landscapes, ritual movements, mystical effects.

## Input
- Folklore, legends, dialogue.
- Voice and chant recordings for tone.
- Cultural imagery for AI visuals.

## Output
- Complete Gothic film script.
- AI storyboard sequences.
- Background score and sound cues.
