# Caribbean Gothic Movie AI Framework

## Core Objective
Generate Gothic stories rooted in Caribbean folklore: Obeah, sea spirits, slavery echoes.

## Features
- Dark magic, ancestral curses, sea spirits.
- Gothic tone-catcher for mood, tension, and emotion.
- AI music: drums, steelpan, ocean waves, ritual sounds.
- Cinematic AI visuals: islands, ports, tropical storms.

## Input
- Text: folklore, scripts.
- Voice and cultural chants.
- Imagery: landscapes, rituals.

## Output
- Full Gothic scripts.
- AI storyboard sequences.
- Integrated adaptive soundtrack.
