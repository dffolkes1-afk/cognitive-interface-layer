# Media Engine AI

## Core Objective
Provide a unified media engine for generating audio-visual content across all AI layers.

## Features
- AI-driven video and audio production.
- Soundtrack and ambient sound generation.
- Video editing and scene composition.
- Integration with storylines and narrative AI layers.
- Adaptive cinematic tone-catcher for emotion calibration.

## Input
- Story scripts, storyboards.
- Audio and visual references.
- AI-generated or user-submitted media assets.

## Output
- Fully produced videos with music, sound effects, and cinematic visuals.
- Scene-by-scene adaptive storyboards.
# Media Engine AI Layer

## Purpose
Provide AI-driven media processing, rendering, and distribution across text, image, audio, and video formats.

## Features
- Automatic media creation and editing
- Audio and video synchronization with storyline
- High-resolution rendering for multi-platform deployment
- Integration with Learning AI and Designer AI Layers
- Adaptive media enhancement (color, audio, lighting, textures)

## Input
- Source media files, story scripts, user briefs
- Visual/audio preferences, mood, and style guidance

## Output
- Fully rendered multimedia content
- Optimized files for distribution or AI-driven presentations
# Gothic Movie AI / Storyline Layer

## Purpose
Generate Gothic-style cinematic stories with full audio-visual integration.

## Features
- Storyline & Narrative AI: plot generation, character arcs, thematic depth
- AI Music & Sound Design: background score, harmonies, mood, and sound effects
- AI Voice & Tone Recognition: realistic voice adaptation for multiple accents and tones
- Multimodal Media Integration: visual, audio, and text cohesion

## Input
- Story scripts, folklore, historical/cultural references
- Mood, tone, character profiles

## Output
- Fully rendered cinematic content
- Adaptive audio-visual storytelling for learning, entertainment, and simulation
