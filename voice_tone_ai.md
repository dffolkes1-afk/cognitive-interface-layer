# AI Voice & Tone Recognition Layer

## Core Objective
Enable AI systems to synthesize, recognize, and adapt to multiple voices, accents, and tones for cinematic or educational purposes.

## Features
- Accurate recognition of accents, dialects, and cultural speech patterns.
- Voice synthesis for characters and narrators in stories or lessons.
- Tone adaptation for emotional delivery in content.
- Integration with Media Engine AI and Gothic Movie AI Layers.
- Real-time voice adjustment and adaptive pronunciation.

## Input
- Text scripts, story dialogues, or lesson content.
- Voice references and tone preferences.

## Output
- High-fidelity synthesized speech.
- Dynamic voice profiles for characters and narrators.
- Emotionally resonant vocal delivery for cinematic or educational content.
