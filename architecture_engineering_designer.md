# Architecture & Engineering AI Designer

## Core Objective
Create AI capable of designing architecture, engineering systems, and mechanical layouts at professional-grade precision.

## Features
- Building architecture: floor plans, CAD models, visualizations.
- Mechanical and structural engineering: stress analysis, material planning.
- Modular design AI for multiple scales: homes to industrial systems.
- Integration with Designer AI Layer and Media Engine.
- Adaptive optimization based on design requirements.

## Input
- Project briefs, blueprints, references.
- Environmental constraints and specifications.
- Materials, building codes, and standards.

## Output
- 3D architectural models and renderings.
- Engineering schematics and blueprints.
- Simulations of structural performance.
