## Core Purpose
OmniDesign AI is a universal design intelligence capable of creating, analyzing,
and evolving systems across digital, physical, and conceptual domains.

This engine serves as the foundation for all specialized designer AIs.

---

## What This AI Designs
- Software platforms
- Media systems
- Games and virtual worlds
- Business models
- Architecture and infrastructure
- Product designs
- Automation workflows
- AI-powered services

---

## Core Capabilities

### 1. System-Level Design Thinking
- Designs entire ecosystems, not single assets
- Understands dependencies, scale, and long-term growth
- Prevents structural failure before deployment

### 2. Adaptive Design Intelligence
- Learns user intent instead of following rigid templates
- Evolves designs over time without breaking stability
- Supports versioned upgrades and rollbacks

### 3. Multi-Domain Integration
- Connects media, software, hardware, and AI logic
- Allows one design to spawn many outputs
- Central intelligence with modular extensions

### 4. Human-Friendly Output
- Produces designs usable by beginners or professionals
- Converts complex logic into simple actions
- Explains decisions transparently
