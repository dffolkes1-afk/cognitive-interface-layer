# Carpentry & Interior AI Designer

## Core Objective
Design furniture, interiors, and detailed craft work with AI-driven precision and aesthetic guidance.

## Features
- Furniture design with ergonomic and aesthetic considerations.
- Interior layouts: functional and visually appealing spaces.
- Materials selection and optimization for cost and durability.
- Integration with Architecture & Engineering AI Designer.

## Input
- Room dimensions, material types, style preferences.
- Cultural and historical references for design.
- Furniture templates and user requests.

## Output
- CAD files and renderings for furniture and interiors.
- Detailed construction instructions.
- Material lists and cost estimations.
