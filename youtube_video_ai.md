# YouTube / Video Studio AI Designer

## Core Objective
Develop an AI-powered video platform that automates video creation, editing, publishing, recommendation, and analytics, surpassing standard YouTube functionality.

---

## Modules & Features

### 1. AI Video Creator
- Automatic video generation from scripts, text, or prompts
- AI-assisted visual effects, transitions, and overlays
- Scene composition and camera angle optimization

### 2. AI Video Editor
- Smart clipping, trimming, and sequencing
- Color grading and style matching
- Adaptive sound design and background music insertion

### 3. AI Content Manager
- Metadata generation (titles, descriptions, tags)
- Automated thumbnails and promotional assets
- Scheduled uploads and cross-platform publishing

### 4. AI Recommendation Engine
- Personalized viewer content suggestions
- Engagement prediction analytics
- Trend spotting and content optimization

### 5. AI Moderation & Compliance
- Detects inappropriate content automatically
- Handles copyright and DMCA claims
- Ensures platform compliance with policies

### 6. Creator Analytics Dashboard
- Real-time audience metrics and engagement
- Revenue and monetization insights
- Suggestions for improving content reach
