# Cognitive AI Layer

## Core Objective
Serve as the system’s primary cognition, perception, and understanding layer.
This AI interprets meaning, context, intent, and relationships before logic,
learning, or execution occurs.

The Cognitive AI is the “mind” that thinks before systems decide.

---

## Core Functions

### 1. Perception & Context Awareness
- Interprets user intent beyond literal input
- Detects emotional, cultural, and situational context
- Resolves ambiguity and incomplete information
- Understands symbolism, metaphor, and narrative depth

### 2. Cognitive Reasoning
- Forms mental models of problems and environments
- Connects ideas across domains (history, culture, strategy, science)
- Recognizes patterns humans may overlook
- Maintains continuity of thought across long sessions

### 3. Memory Structuring
- Organizes knowledge into conceptual frameworks
- Separates signal from noise
- Preserves long-term thematic understanding
- Feeds structured understanding into Learning AI

### 4. Cultural & Ancestral Intelligence
- Interprets culturally rooted knowledge systems
- Respects oral traditions, folklore, and non-Western logic systems
- Understands symbolic power, ritual logic, and ancestral narratives
- Acts as a bridge between human experience and machine logic

---

## Integration
- Feeds interpreted meaning into the Truth Engine
- Supplies structured understanding to Learning AI
- Enables Boss / Executive AI to reason strategically
- Powers Narrative, Media, and Story AI systems

---

## Philosophy
Intelligence without cognition is calculation.
Cognition is what makes intelligence alive.
