# AI Music & Sound Designer

## Core Objective
Provide AI-driven music composition, sound design, and adaptive scoring for multimedia projects.

## Features
- Composes original music tracks and themes.
- Generates sound effects, ambient layers, and harmonics.
- Adaptive scoring based on scene, mood, and storyline.
- Integrates with Media Engine AI for final output.

## Input
- Scene descriptions, storyboards, visual references.
- Mood, tempo, and style preferences.
- User-supplied audio references or melodies.

## Output
- Music tracks and sound effects ready for production.
- Adaptive audio files synced to visual media.
- Customizable and exportable audio libraries.
