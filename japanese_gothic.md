# Japanese Gothic Movie AI Framework

## Core Objective
Establish a Gothic story generator based on Japanese folklore, ninja clans, and Onmyōdō mysticism. Integrates AI-driven music, ambient sound, and visual pacing for cinematic effect.

## Features
- Blood oath plotlines and clan rivalries.
- Dark atmospheric settings: castles, forests, shrines.
- Emotionally calibrated AI tone-catcher for horror, tension, and suspense.
- Adaptive sound design: taiko drums, bamboo flutes, wind, water, ritual sounds.
- Visual cinematic AI engine: camera movement, lighting, shadow play.

## Input
- Text: folklore, scripts, dialogue.
- Audio: voice recordings for tone reference.
- Video/Image: reference shots for visual mood.

## Output
- Full Gothic movie script.
- Scene-by-scene storyboard with AI visuals.
- Integrated soundtrack and sound effects.
