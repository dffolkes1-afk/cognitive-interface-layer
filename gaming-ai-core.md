# Gaming AI Core

## Overview
The Gaming AI module manages intelligent game design, player interaction, and adaptive content generation. It ensures fully immersive, dynamic, and evolving gaming experiences for Mintage.

## Components
1. **Procedural Content Engine** – Generates levels, missions, and scenarios dynamically.
2. **Adaptive NPC AI** – Creates non-player characters that evolve and respond intelligently to player behavior.
3. **Player Behavior Analytics** – Monitors player actions to optimize game balance and personalization.
4. **Multi-Platform Integration** – Connects gaming AI across desktop, mobile, AR/VR, and cloud systems.
5. **Community & Collaboration Layer** – Supports AI-driven game design collaboration with other designers and modules.

## Future Enhancements
- Real-time cross-game AI evolution
- AI-driven storylines and character arcs
- Integration with media, video, and music AI modules
