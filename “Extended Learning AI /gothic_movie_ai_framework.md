# Gothic Movie AI Framework

## Purpose
AI-driven system for generating original Gothic-style movies and cinematic stories from existing cultural narratives, legends, and folklore.

## Features
1. **Story Import & Analysis**
   - Accepts text, summaries, scripts, or folklore as input.
   - Analyzes plot, characters, themes, and cultural tone.

2. **Gothic Transformation Engine**
   - Rewrites source material into Gothic cinematic style.
   - Adds darkness, emotional depth, suspense, and extreme scenarios.
   - Maintains cultural accuracy while enhancing cinematic intensity.

3. **Spin-off Generator**
   - Creates new characters, villains, or parallel storylines.
   - Expands original universe while keeping tone consistent.

4. **Cultural Story Layers**
   - Supports stories from multiple origins:  
     - Japanese ninja tales  
     - Indian folklore  
     - Black American cultural narratives  
     - Chinese legends  
   - Can combine story elements or cross-cultural mashups.

5. **Script & Storyboard Output**
   - Formats output as movie scripts, storyboard outlines, or cinematic narratives.
   - Ready for visual adaptation.

6. **AI Control Panel**
   - Adjustable parameters for tone, level of graphic content, pacing, and emotional intensity.
   - Scheduling system to queue stories and spin-offs.

## Usage
1. Upload or input source material.  
2. Set desired Gothic intensity, emotional depth, and graphic level.  
3. AI generates transformed story, spin-offs, and cinematic scripts.  
4. Review and adjust output as needed.  
5. Store in repository for further expansion or visualization.
